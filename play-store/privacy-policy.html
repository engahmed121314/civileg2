1. Introduction

Civil Engineer Pro is a structural engineering design and calculation tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data. This app is designed with a privacy-first approach — it operates primarily offline and does not require user registration or account creation.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Design Input Data: Structural dimensions, material properties, and load values you enter for engineering calculations. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Project Names: Any project names or labels you create for organizing your work — stored locally only.
  • App Settings: Your preferred language, design code (ECP/ACI/SBC), theme, and unit system preferences — stored locally only.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • App Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app's performance and user experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Device Information: For compatibility and crash reporting, we may collect device model, Android version, and crash logs. No personal identifiers are included.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number) through the app.
  • We do NOT track your location.
  • We do NOT access your contacts, camera, microphone, or any other device sensors.
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Use Information

All data collected is used solely for:

  • Performing structural engineering calculations on your device
  • Generating PDF/Excel design reports
  • Saving and organizing your design projects locally
  • Improving app stability through anonymous crash reports
  • Providing app updates and improvements

4. Data Storage and Security

All your design data is stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • ProGuard/R8 code obfuscation to prevent reverse engineering
  • Tamper detection and integrity verification
  • Secure file storage with restricted access
  • Backup and data extraction restrictions
  • Network security configuration blocking cleartext traffic

5. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: For potential future API integrations and update checks only. No data is sent without your explicit consent.
  • Storage (Read/Write): To save exported PDF/Excel reports and design files to your device. On Android 13+, scoped storage is used for minimal access.
  • Network State: To check internet connectivity for online features.

6. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms that share data with advertisers, or social media platforms. The app operates as a self-contained engineering tool.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is a professional engineering tool and is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing the app's data through your device's Settings > Apps > Civil Engineer Pro > Storage > Clear Data. Upon uninstalling the app, all locally stored data is automatically removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your design data stored on your device
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Export your designs in PDF/Excel format
  • Opt out of any analytics by disabling network access for this app
  • Contact the developer with privacy-related inquiries

11. Disclaimer

This application is provided for educational and preliminary design purposes only. All structural calculations should be verified by a licensed structural engineer. The developer is not responsible for any errors, damages, or liability resulting from the use of this application.
