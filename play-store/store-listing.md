## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Civil Engineer Pro is a comprehensive structural engineering design application built for professional engineers, students, and construction professionals. Designed with precision and ease of use in mind, it supports multiple international design codes and provides instant, accurate calculations for all major structural elements.

🔍 DESIGN MODULES:
• Beam Design — Simply supported, continuous, cantilever, and doubly reinforced beams
• Column Design — Rectangular and circular columns with interaction diagrams
• Slab Design — Solid, flat, waffle, and ribbed slabs (one-way & two-way)
• Foundation Design — Isolated and combined footings with soil bearing capacity checks
• Staircase Design — Single flight, double flight, and cantilever stairs
• Retaining Wall Design — Cantilever retaining walls with stability checks
• Water Tank Design — Rectangular and circular tanks (elevated & underground)
• Steel Design — Steel warehouse structures and steel connections
• Frame Analysis — 2D portal frame analysis with moment and shear diagrams
• Seismic Analysis — Earthquake load calculations per design codes

📋 DESIGN CODES SUPPORTED:
• ECP 203 (Egyptian Code for Concrete Structures)
• ACI 318 (American Concrete Institute)
• SBC (Saudi Building Code)

📄 PROFESSIONAL REPORTS:
Export detailed PDF design reports including:
• Complete input parameters and assumptions
• Step-by-step design equations with code references
• Detailed cross-section drawings showing reinforcement layout
• Moment, shear, and load diagrams
• Bill of Quantities (BOQ) with cost estimation

🔧 ADDITIONAL FEATURES:
• Bilingual interface (Arabic & English)
• Unit converter (SI & Imperial)
• Rebar inventory management
• Project vault for saving and organizing designs
• Scientific calculator
• Cost estimation with customizable material prices
• Dark/Light theme support
• Works offline — no internet required for calculations
